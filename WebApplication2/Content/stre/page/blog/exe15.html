Care sunt punctele cheie pentru o excutare corecta a exercitiului?
  • Squeeze your shoulder blades down and back to create a solid platform between you and the bench
  • Set up with your feet directly below or behind your knees so that you can create tension through the lower body by driving them into the floor
  • Grip the bar just outside shoulder width so that as you make contact with your chest your formarms and elbows are straight under the bar
  • Lower the bar under control by tucking your elbows to about 45 degrees, making contact around the sternum area
  • Finish the movement by punching the bar back up to the start position
